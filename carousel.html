The Neighborhood of Pelham Bay

Pelham Bay is a mostly residential neighborhood where the transportation access of Amendola Plaza, the natural beauty and amenities of Pelham Bay Park, and a small-neighborhood feel combine. The community is committed to preserving the neighborhood's history and character and desire that future investments match with the area's current density. The recent formation of a Business Improvement District is intended to support the existing stock of mostly small businesses in the area and maintain the quality and diversity of service, shopping, and dining establishtments available to the residents, commuters, and tourists who spend time in Pelham Bay.
